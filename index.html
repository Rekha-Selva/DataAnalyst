Power BIDAXExcelSelf-Directed
Road Accident Analysis Dashboard
Overview

Built a comprehensive Power BI casualties report analysing 58,000+ UK road accident records across 2021 and 2022 — delivering a fully interactive dashboard tracking fatal, serious, and slight casualties with year-over-year trend comparison, vehicle type breakdown, road type analysis, and geographic hotspot mapping.

What I Built
  • 5 executive KPI cards — Total Casualties, Total Accidents, Fatal, Serious, and Slight — each with YoY % change vs prior year
  • CY vs PY casualties trend line chart comparing monthly 2021 and 2022 performance across the full calendar year
  • Casualties by Vehicle Type panel — Agricultural, Bike, Bus, Car, Other, Van — with counts and custom vehicle icons
  • Casualties by Road Type horizontal bar chart — Single carriageway (145K), Dual carriageway, Roundabout, One-way street, Slip road
  • Casualties by Area donut — Urban 61.95% vs Rural 38.05% split
  • Casualties by Light Condition donut — daylight vs darkness breakdown
  • UK geographic map (Microsoft Bing) with accident density hotspots across England, Scotland and Wales
  • DAX measures for YoY variance, current year totals, and prior year comparisons across all KPIs
🔑 Key Insight

Total casualties fell 11.9% YoY from 2021 to 2022, with fatal casualties showing the sharpest decline at 33.3% — while single carriageways remained by far the most dangerous road type at 145K casualties, representing over 74% of all incidents.
